Sky Ranch- The Sky Ranch Tagaytay amusement park is one of the most visited Tagaytay tourist spots because of all the thrilling rides and Instagrammable attractions. Paradizoo Tagaytay- If you want to visit some cute animals during your trip, stop by Paradizoo Tagaytay! It may not be as popular as other Tagaytay tourist spots, but it’s the only theme park and zoo concept in Tagaytay. Our Lady of Manaoag, Tierra de Maria- Seek refuge in this peaceful shrine of Our Lady of Manaoag in Tagaytay. Located along Tagaytay-Calamba Road, Our Lady of Manaoag, Tierra de Maria is the place to be when you want to set aside time to reflect, meditate, and marvel at age-old artifacts, paintings, and sculptures.
